About Us

Supersonic Tortoises are a robotics team participating in the FIRST Team Competition, which is a global robotics competition in which thousands of teams from around the world use their skills, in both construction and programming, to create a fully-functioning robot that performs a specific task. In order to give back to the community, Supersonic Tortoises has organized many events to spread awareness about FTC and robotics as a whole. This hackathon is one such event, in which we hope you will partake!
